Our Journey

From foundation to excellence - the milestones that shaped COMPAQ BUILDERS

2008

The Foundation

COMPAQ BUILDERS was founded with a vision to transform the construction industry through quality craftsmanship and innovative solutions. Started with a small team of dedicated professionals.

2010

First Major Project

Completed our first major residential complex, establishing our reputation for quality construction and timely delivery. This project became a benchmark for our future endeavors.

2013

Expansion & Growth

Expanded our services to include commercial construction and infrastructure development. Grew our team to 50+ professionals and acquired advanced construction equipment.

2016

Innovation Focus

Introduced sustainable construction practices and modern building technologies. Became pioneers in eco-friendly construction solutions in our region.

2019

Award Recognition

Received multiple industry awards for excellence in construction and project management. Recognized as one of the top construction companies in the region.

2024

Future Forward

Today, we continue to innovate and expand, with over 500 successful projects and a team committed to building the future. Leading the way in smart construction technologies.
